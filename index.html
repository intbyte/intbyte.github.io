Typing Tool
The typing tool you need to decorate your game. This tool will lessen that burden of implementing algorithms and thinking. Download this item on Unity Asset Store and speed up your work. This asset contains various effects of typing. They are "Normal typing", "Sci-fi typing", "Custom", "Random" etc. and their reverse. The main advantage is all the settings are customizable. Just add 'TypingMain.cs' to a UI gameobject and customize the setting according to your need.

B&W: Post Processing
Want to make your game retro type or need a vignette effect? Here is the asset you are looking for. Just attach this component to your Camera gameObject and customize the settings. Hit the play button and see the result. The main thing is that you can get this asset from Unity Asset Store for free. This package also includes Black and White Camera effect. So download this asset from Unity Asset Store for free and boost up your development process.
